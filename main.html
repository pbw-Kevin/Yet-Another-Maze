So since you see these words, I will gladly tell you that this is not a simple maze. In fact, a pair of portals is in two blocks of this maze. Specificially, when you step into one of the portals, you will be teleported to the other, with the same direction.

For example, if you step into one portal from the left, you will come out from and be in the right of the other portal. In other words, you will never be really IN the portal block. You only start from and end up in the block next to the portal.
